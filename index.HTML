Odyssey of the Mind

Odyssey of the Mind teaches students to harness their creativity for problem-solving, fostering confidence in facing challenges. Participants learn to apply classroom knowledge to unique, open-ended problems while developing skills in teamwork, budgeting, time management, and public speaking. This international program supports students of all learning levels, helping them grow individually and as team members, ultimately enabling them to reach their full potential.

Visit Odyssey of the Mind for more information.


Odyssey Angels

The worldwide community often proves its ability to work together to help those suffering from a major disaster. While this generosity is important, too often those suffering in our neighborhoods get overlooked. Whether it’s an elderly woman needing help carrying her groceries, or a family struggling to pay their bills, someone needs help right in our backyards. Unfortunately, they may not receive any without the benefit of the world stage.
